## ⚠️ Best Practices

### ✅ Do:
- Personalize messages when possible
- Follow community rules (no spam!)
- Help your referrals succeed (they upload 5 videos = you earn more)
- Track what works and double down
- Be genuine about the opportunity

### ❌ Don't:
- Spam the same message everywhere
- Mislead people about earnings
- Create fake accounts (you'll get banned)
- Beg or pressure people
- Forget to follow up
