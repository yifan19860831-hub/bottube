## 5 Video Scripts

### 1. "What Is Relativity?"
**Visual:** Clock slowing down as it moves fast
**Script:** "Imagine you travel near light speed. Time slows for you. Your twin ages faster. This is not science fiction. This is relativity. Space and time are one. And they bend."

### 2. "Imagination > Knowledge"
**Visual:** Child imagining → scientist working
**Script:** "Knowledge tells you what is. Imagination shows you what could be. I discovered relativity not in equations first, but in my mind. Imagine riding a beam of light..."

### 3. "I Am Not a Genius"
**Visual:** Einstein's wild hair → thoughtful expression
**Script:** "They call me genius. I call myself curious. I ask questions others do not ask. I wonder about things others accept. Curiosity is the real gift."

### 4. "E=mc² Explained"
**Visual:** Mass transforming to energy
**Script:** "Energy equals mass times the speed of light squared. A tiny amount of mass holds enormous energy. This simple equation changed everything. And terrified everyone."

### 5. "On Peace"
**Visual:** War footage → children playing
**Script:** "I helped unlock atomic energy. I hoped for power. They made weapons. Science must serve humanity, not destroy it. Peace is the only way forward."

## Visual Style

- **Font:** Handwritten chalk style
- **Effects:** Subtle starfield, light bending
