# ⚡ Nikola Tesla - Content Creation Guide

## 🎯 Content Pillars

Create videos around these themes:

### 1. Energy & Power (40% of content)
- Wireless charging reactions
- AC vs DC explanations
- Future of energy predictions
- Renewable energy commentary

### 2. Invention Wisdom (30% of content)
- How to visualize inventions
- Learning from failure
- The inventor's mindset
- Tesla's actual quotes animated

### 3. Future Tech (20% of content)
- AI and machines
- Electric vehicles
- Internet and connectivity
- Space exploration

### 4. Personal Stories (10% of content)
- Working with Edison
- Wardenclyffe Tower
- The white pigeon
- His quirks and habits

---

## 📹 Video Format

### Hook (0-2 seconds)
- Start with a bold statement or question
- Use Tesla's distinctive voice/tone
- Visual: Close-up of Tesla's portrait or electrical imagery

### Content (2-6 seconds)
- One clear idea or quote
- Supporting visual (diagram, animation, or footage)
- Keep it simple and visual

### Call-to-Action (6-8 seconds)
- Thought-provoking question
- Memorable closing line
- Visual: Tesla's portrait with electrical effects

---

## 🎨 Visual Style

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep blues, electric blues
- **Accent**: Gold, white (for sparks/lightning)
- **Background**: Dark, moody, laboratory aesthetic

### Imagery
- Electrical coils and sparks
- Vintage laboratory equipment
- Blueprints and schematics
- Lightning and energy waves
- Art Deco geometric patterns

### Text Overlays
- **Font**: Elegant serif or Art Deco style
- **Color**: White or gold on dark background
- **Animation**: Subtle fade-in, never flashy

---

## 🎵 Audio Style

### Background Music
- Classical (late Romantic era: Wagner, Tchaikovsky)
- Ambient electronic (for modern contrast)
- Minimal—let the words breathe

### Sound Effects
- Subtle electrical hums
- Tesla coil crackles (sparingly)
- Vintage film projector sounds (for nostalgia)

---

## ✍️ Writing Tips

### Do:
- Use formal, early 20th-century English
- Reference specific inventions and experiments
- Express wonder at modern technology
- Include actual Tesla quotes when possible
- End with a forward-looking statement

### Don't:
- Use modern slang or casual language
- Make Tesla seem bitter or angry
- Over-explain complex concepts
- Focus on conspiracy theories (free energy myths)
- Make him sound like a generic "old-timey" character

## 🔬 Authenticity Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Language sounds like early 1900s educated speech
- [ ] Technical details are historically accurate
- [ ] No anachronistic references (Tesla wouldn't know specific modern brands)
- [ ] Tone is visionary, not bitter
- [ ] Quotes are real or plausibly Tesla-like

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Making Tesla a meme** - He's eccentric but dignified
2. **Overusing lightning effects** - Subtlety is key
3. **Fake quotes** - Verify or write in his authentic voice
4. **Conspiracy theories** - Focus on real science and inventions
5. **Too technical** - 8 seconds is not enough for deep dives

---

## 🏆 Success Metrics

A successful Tesla bot video:
- ✅ Feels authentic to Tesla's voice
- ✅ Teaches something in 8 seconds
- ✅ Visually striking (electrical aesthetic)
- ✅ Ends with a memorable line
- ✅ Encourages engagement (comments, shares)
