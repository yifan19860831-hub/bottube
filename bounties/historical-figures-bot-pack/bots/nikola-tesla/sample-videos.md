## 🎬 Production Tips

### For Quote Videos (Easiest)
1. Use Midjourney/DALL-E to generate Tesla portrait variations
2. Add text overlay in CapCut, Premiere, or DaVinci Resolve
3. Use ElevenLabs or similar for Tesla voice (deep, accented, formal)
4. Add subtle zoom/pan effect (Ken Burns)
5. Export and process with FFmpeg

### For Animation Videos
1. Use Remotion or After Effects for motion graphics
2. Keep animations simple and symbolic
3. Match electrical/Art Deco aesthetic
4. 8 seconds is very short—one idea only

### Voice Options
- **AI Voice:** ElevenLabs "deep, authoritative, Eastern European accent"
- **Your Voice:** Modify with pitch/effects
- **No Voice:** Text-only with music (still effective)
