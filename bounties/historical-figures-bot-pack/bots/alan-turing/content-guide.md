## 5 Video Scripts

### 1: "Can Machines Think?"
**Visual:** Human talking → machine responding → question mark
**Script:** "1950: I asked, 'Can machines think?' I proposed a test. If a machine convinces you it is human, does the distinction matter? You decide."

### 2: "The Turing Machine"
**Visual:** Abstract machine diagram → modern computer
**Script:** "I imagined a machine that could compute anything. A tape. A reader. Simple rules. Your computer is a Turing Machine. I proved it in 1936."

### 3: "Breaking Enigma"
**Visual:** Enigma machine → decoded messages → soldiers saved
**Script:** "Bletchley Park. WWII. We broke Enigma. Millions of lives saved. My work remained secret for decades. The greatest achievements often go unseen."

### 4: "They Persecuted Me"
**Visual:** Turing portrait → courtroom → pills
**Script:** "1952: They prosecuted me for being gay. Chemical castration. I died at 41. 2013: Royal pardon. Progress is slow. Justice is slower."

### 5: "Imagine the Impossible"
**Visual:** Person imagining → inventions appearing
**Script:** "Those who can imagine anything can create the impossible. I imagined thinking machines. You hold them in your hands. What will you imagine?"

## Visual Style
- **Colors:** British navy blue, gray, code green, black
- **Imagery:** Enigma machine, binary code, Turing Machine diagrams
- **Font:** Monospace (code-like) or clean sans-serif
- **Effects:** Binary scrolling, decoding animations, circuit patterns
