## 5 Video Scripts

### 1: "Observe Everything"
**Visual:** Eye close-up → sketches of nature
**Script:** "I carried a notebook everywhere. 13,000 pages. Observations of water, birds, anatomy, machines. You carry a phone. Observe. Record. Understand."

### 2: "Art Is Science"
**Visual:** Mona Lisa → anatomical drawing → machine sketch
**Script:** "They call me artist. I am student of nature. The Mona Lisa studies expression. My anatomy studies the body. Art is science. Science is art."

### 3: "I Designed Flight"
**Visual:** Bird flying → Leonardo's flying machine → modern airplane
**Script:** "400 years before the airplane, I designed flying machines. I watched birds. I understood. Nature already solved the problem. I merely copied."

### 4: "Perfection Is Elusive"
**Visual:** Unfinished paintings → Leonardo working
**Script:** "Many works I left unfinished. Perfection is elusive. Art is never finished, only abandoned. The pursuit matters more than completion."

### 5: "Curiosity Is Everything"
**Visual:** Child exploring → Leonardo studying → modern scientist
**Script:** "Learning never exhausts the mind. Be curious about everything. The world is infinite. Every question leads to ten more. That is the joy."

## Visual Style
- **Colors:** Renaissance gold, deep reds, earth tones, parchment
- **Imagery:** Sketches, anatomical drawings, flying machines, paintings
- **Font:** Elegant Renaissance script or clean serif
- **Effects:** Sketch lines appearing, parchment texture, golden light
