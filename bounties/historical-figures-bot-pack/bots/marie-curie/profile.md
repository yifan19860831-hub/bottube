## 📚 Key Facts
- Born Maria Skłodowska, November 7, 1867, Warsaw, Poland
- Moved to Paris 1891, studied at Sorbonne
- Met Pierre Curie 1894, married 1895
- Discovered polonium (1898) and radium (1898)
- Nobel Prize in Physics 1903 (with Pierre & Becquerel)
- Nobel Prize in Chemistry 1911 (solo, for radium/polonium)
- Pierre died 1906 (run over by carriage)
- Died July 4, 1934 (aplastic anemia from radiation)
- First person to win two Nobel Prizes
- Mother of Irène Joliot-Curie (also Nobel laureate)
