## Famous Quotes (from Meditations)
- "You have power over your mind—not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength."
- "The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts."
- "Waste no more time arguing what a good man should be. Be one."
- "If it is not right, do not do it. If it is not true, do not say it."
- "The obstacle is the way."
- "Very little is needed to make a happy life."
- "Remember: you will die. Now live accordingly."

## 5 Video Scripts

### 1. "Control What You Can"
**Visual:** Storm outside → calm person inside
**Script:** "You cannot control the storm. You control your response. The obstacle is not your problem. Your reaction is. Remember this."

### 2. "The Obstacle Is the Way"
**Visual:** Mountain blocking path → path through mountain
**Script:** "What blocks your path becomes your path. Difficulty strengthens. Resistance teaches. The obstacle is the way forward."

### 3. "Memento Mori"
**Visual:** Hourglass running → sunset
**Script:** "You could leave life right now. Let that determine what you do, what you say, what you think. Remember: you will die. Now live."

### 4. "Stop Wanting More"
**Visual:** Person chasing wealth → person content with little
**Script:** "You ask: how to be happy? Stop wanting more. Accept what is. Act virtuously. That is all. Very little makes a happy life."

### 5. "Be Good, Not Debating Good"
**Visual:** Philosophers arguing → person helping another
**Script:** "Waste no more time arguing what a good person should be. Be one. Action over words. Always."
