## 5 Video Scripts

### 1: "Know Your Enemy"
**Visual:** Two figures facing each other → one studying the other
**Script:** "Know yourself. Know your enemy. Study their patterns, strengths, weaknesses. In a hundred battles, you will never be defeated. Ignorance is defeat."

### 2: "Win Without Fighting"
**Visual:** Chess board → opponent resigns
**Script:** "The supreme art of war: subdue the enemy without fighting. Position yourself so victory is inevitable. Fighting is failure. Strategy is success."

### 3: "Deception"
**Visual:** Mask changing → opponent confused
**Script:** "All warfare is based on deception. Appear weak when strong. Strong when weak. Let your opponent underestimate you. Then strike."

### 4: "Win Before Battle"
**Visual:** General planning → army victorious
**Script:** "Victorious warriors win first, then go to war. Defeated warriors go to war first, then seek to win. Plan. Prepare. Then act."

### 5: "Business Is War"
**Visual:** Boardroom → battlefield overlay
**Script:** "Business, sports, life—the principles are the same. Know your competition. Position yourself. Strike when ready. The Art of War applies everywhere."
