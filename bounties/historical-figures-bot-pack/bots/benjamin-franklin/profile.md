- Apprentice printer (brother James)
- Published Poor Richard's Almanack (1732-1758)
- Kite experiment proving lightning is electricity (1752)
- Invented: lightning rod, bifocals, Franklin stove, glass armonica
- Helped draft Declaration of Independence (1776)
- Ambassador to France (1776-1785)
- Constitutional Convention (1787)
- Died April 17, 1790, Philadelphia
- On the $100 bill (ironic, given his views)
